Disrupting 9 to 5

The pandemic has served as a reminder that we often get caught up in the daily hustle of achieving things that may not hold much meaning in the grand scheme of things. By constantly running forward with blinders on, we miss out on the simple joys of everyday life and being present in the moment.

However, at Blob, we have embraced the disruptions caused by the pandemic. The newfound flexibility of location and timing has allowed us to redesign our lives in a way that aligns with our values. Our offerings at Blob are designed to help individuals make the most of this newfound freedom and live each day to the fullest.
